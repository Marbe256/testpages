# 数式の例

インライン数式: $\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i - \mu)^2}$

ディスプレイ数式:

$$
\Sigma = \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i
$$

ギリシャ文字: $\alpha, \beta, \gamma, \sigma, \Sigma$

$$
\Sigma^{1} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k = \frac{n^2}{2}-\frac{n}{2}
$$

$$
\Sigma^{2} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k^2 = \frac{n^3}{3}-\frac{n^2}{2}+\frac{n}{6}
$$

$$
\Sigma^{1,1} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k(n-k) = n\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k - \sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k^2 = \frac{n^3}{6}-\frac{n}{6}
$$

$$
\Sigma^{3} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k^3 = \frac{n^4}{4}-\frac{n^3}{2}+\frac{n^2}{4}
$$

$$
\Sigma^{4} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k^4 = \frac{n^5}{5}-\frac{n^4}{2}+\frac{n^3}{3}-\frac{n^2}{12}
$$

$$
\Sigma^{5} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k^5 = \frac{n^6}{6}-\frac{n^5}{2}+\frac{n^4}{4}-\frac{n^3}{12}+\frac{n^2}{20}
$$

$$
\Sigma^{6} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k^6 = \frac{n^7}{7}-\frac{n^6}{2}+\frac{n^5}{5}-\frac{n^4}{12}+\frac{n^3}{20}-\frac{n^2}{30}
$$


$$
\Sigma^{7} =\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k^7 = \frac{n^8}{8}-\frac{n^7}{2}+\frac{n^6}{6}-\frac{n^5}{12}+\frac{n^4}{20}-\frac{n^3}{30}+\frac{n^2}{42}
$$
